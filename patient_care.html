Patient Rights

At Arvindu Hospitals, we respect and protect the rights of all our patients:

  • Right to quality healthcare and treatment
  • Right to informed consent and participation in treatment decisions
  • Right to privacy and confidentiality of medical information
  • Right to respectful and dignified treatment
  • Right to access medical records and information
  • Right to voice complaints and receive prompt resolution

Patient Responsibilities

We also ask our patients to help us provide the best care by:

  • Providing accurate and complete health information
  • Following prescribed treatment plans and instructions
  • Keeping scheduled appointments or providing advance notice
  • Treating staff and other patients with respect
  • Meeting financial obligations in a timely manner
  • Following hospital policies and safety guidelines

Visiting Information

Important information for patients and visitors

Visiting Hours

  • General Wards: 4:00 PM - 7:00 PM
  • ICU: 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Pediatric Ward: 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM
  • Maternity Ward: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Visitor Guidelines

  • Maximum 2 visitors per patient at a time
  • Children under 12 years require adult supervision
  • Visitors must register at the reception desk
  • Mobile phones should be on silent mode
  • Follow all safety and hygiene protocols
