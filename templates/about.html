Learn about our journey, mission, vision, and values that drive our commitment to healthcare excellence.

Our Story

Arvindu Hospitals was established in 2005 with a vision to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to the communities of Bihar. What started as a small clinic in Patna has now expanded to three major branches across Patna, Chapra, and Jamui.

Over the years, we have grown into a multi-specialty hospital with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of experienced medical professionals. Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement, expansion of services, and implementation of the latest medical technologies.

Today, Arvindu Hospitals stands as a testament to our commitment to healthcare excellence and our dedication to serving the people of Bihar with compassion and integrity.

Our Mission

At Arvindu Hospitals, our mission is to deliver exceptional healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and excellence. We strive to:

  • Provide accessible and affordable healthcare to all sections of society
  • Deliver patient-centered care that respects the diversity, dignity, and needs of each individual
  • Maintain the highest standards of medical practice and ethics
  • Continuously improve our services through innovation and research
  • Create a supportive environment for our healthcare professionals to excel

Our Vision

We envision Arvindu Hospitals as:

  • A leading healthcare institution recognized for excellence in patient care
  • A center of medical innovation and advanced treatments
  • A place where patients feel cared for and respected
  • A nurturing environment for healthcare professionals to grow and develop
  • A pillar of support for community health and wellness

Our vision is to transform healthcare delivery in Bihar by setting new standards of medical excellence and compassionate care.

Our Core Values
