Welcome to Arvindu Hospitals

Established in 2005, Arvindu Hospitals has grown to become one of the leading multi-specialty healthcare providers in Bihar with three branches in Patna, Chapra, and Jamui. Our mission is to deliver excellent medical services with compassion and care.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a team of highly qualified doctors, we are committed to providing the highest standard of medical care to our patients. We believe in a patient-centered approach and strive to make healthcare accessible to all.

Our dedicated team of medical professionals works tirelessly to ensure that each patient receives personalized attention and the best possible treatment. We continuously invest in advanced medical technologies and training to stay at the forefront of healthcare innovation.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare provider, recognized for clinical excellence, compassionate patient care, and innovative medical solutions. We aspire to improve the health and well-being of the communities we serve and set new standards in healthcare delivery.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services with compassion and integrity. We are committed to patient safety, clinical excellence, and continuous improvement. Our goal is to enhance the health and quality of life of our patients through dedicated service and advanced medical care.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a leading healthcare provider

2005 - The Beginning

Arvindu Hospitals was established in Patna with a small team of dedicated doctors and a vision to provide quality healthcare services to the community. The initial facility had 50 beds and basic medical departments.

2010 - Expansion

Due to growing demand, Arvindu Hospitals expanded its facilities and added new specialties. The bed capacity was increased to 100, and advanced diagnostic equipment was installed to enhance the quality of care.

2015 - New Branches

Arvindu Hospitals opened two new branches in Chapra and Jamui to extend its services to more communities. Each facility was equipped with modern medical technology and staffed with skilled healthcare professionals.

2020 - Modern Healthcare

The hospital underwent a digital transformation with the implementation of electronic medical records and telemedicine services. New departments were added, and existing ones were upgraded with cutting-edge equipment.
