About Arvindu Hospitals

Established with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare, Arvindu Hospitals has grown to become one of the leading healthcare providers in the region with branches in Patna, Chapra, and Jamui.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and excellence. We strive to create a healing environment that respects the diversity, dignity, and special needs of each individual.
