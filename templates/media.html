July 15, 2023

Arvindu Hospitals Launches State-of-the-Art Cardiac Care Center

Arvindu Hospitals is proud to announce the inauguration of our new Comprehensive Cardiac Care Center at our Patna facility. This state-of-the-art center is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by leading cardiologists to provide advanced cardiac care to patients across Bihar.

The center features a modern Cath Lab, advanced diagnostic equipment, dedicated cardiac ICU, and specialized cardiac rehabilitation services. This expansion reflects our commitment to addressing the growing cardiovascular health needs in our community and ensuring that patients have access to world-class cardiac care without having to travel to metropolitan cities.
