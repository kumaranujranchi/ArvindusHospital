Patient-Centered Care

We are committed to providing compassionate and personalized care

Our Approach to Patient Care

At Arvindu Hospitals, we believe that every patient deserves personalized attention and care. Our patient-centered approach focuses on treating not just the illness but the whole person, taking into account individual needs, preferences, and values.

We strive to create a healing environment where patients feel respected, informed, and involved in their care decisions. Our teams of healthcare professionals work collaboratively to ensure that each patient receives comprehensive care of the highest quality.

From admission to discharge and follow-up, we aim to make your healthcare journey as comfortable and stress-free as possible. Our dedicated staff is available around the clock to address your concerns and provide support when you need it most.

Patient Services

Comprehensive services designed to meet your healthcare needs

Inpatient Services

Our inpatient services provide 24/7 nursing care, comfortable accommodations, and comprehensive medical attention for patients requiring overnight stays. We offer private, semi-private, and general ward options with amenities designed for comfort and recovery.

Outpatient Services

Our outpatient services offer convenient access to medical care without hospitalization. We provide consultations, diagnostic tests, minor procedures, and follow-up care on a same-day basis, allowing patients to return home after treatment.

Emergency Care

Our emergency department is staffed with experienced physicians and nurses ready to provide immediate care for critical conditions. We utilize advanced technology and follow established protocols to deliver prompt and effective emergency treatment.

Diagnostic Services

Our comprehensive diagnostic services include laboratory testing, medical imaging (X-ray, Ultrasound, CT, MRI), cardiac diagnostics, and other specialized tests. Our state-of-the-art equipment ensures accurate results for proper diagnosis and treatment planning.

Surgical Services

Our surgical services encompass pre-operative evaluation, intra-operative care, and post-operative recovery. Our surgeons are skilled in various procedures, from minimally invasive techniques to complex surgeries, always prioritizing patient safety and outcomes.

Preventive Care

Our preventive care services focus on maintaining good health and preventing illness. We offer health screenings, immunizations, health education, and lifestyle counseling to help patients stay healthy and detect potential issues early.

Patient Education

Empowering patients with knowledge for better health outcomes

Understanding Your Medical Condition

Knowledge about your medical condition helps you make informed decisions about your care. Our healthcare providers take time to explain diagnoses, treatment options, and expected outcomes in clear, understandable terms. We encourage patients to ask questions and participate actively in their care planning.

Medication Management

Understanding your medications is crucial for effective treatment. Our healthcare team provides detailed information about prescribed medications, including purpose, dosage, timing, potential side effects, and important precautions. Our pharmacists are available to address any questions or concerns about your medications.

Preventive Care Guidelines

Prevention is better than cure. We provide guidance on preventive health measures, including recommended screenings based on age, gender, and risk factors. Our health education materials cover topics like nutrition, exercise, stress management, and lifestyle modifications to prevent disease and promote wellness.

Discharge Instructions

Clear discharge instructions are essential for continued recovery at home. Before leaving the hospital, patients receive comprehensive instructions about follow-up care, medication schedules, activity restrictions, dietary guidelines, potential complications to watch for, and when to seek medical attention. Our staff ensures you understand these instructions fully.

Patient Rights & Responsibilities

Understanding your rights and responsibilities as a patient

Your Rights as a Patient

  • Right to Respectful Care: You have the right to considerate, respectful care regardless of your race, religion, gender, ethnicity, or source of payment.
  • Right to Information: You have the right to clear information about your diagnosis, treatment options, and prognosis in terms you can understand.
  • Right to Informed Consent: You have the right to be involved in decisions about your care and to provide informed consent before any procedure.
  • Right to Privacy: You have the right to privacy regarding your medical care and medical records.
  • Right to Confidentiality: You have the right to expect that all communications and records pertaining to your care will be treated as confidential.
  • Right to Refuse Treatment: You have the right to refuse treatment to the extent permitted by law and to be informed of the medical consequences of such refusal.

Your Responsibilities as a Patient

  • Provide Information: You are responsible for providing accurate and complete information about your health, including past illnesses, hospitalizations, medications, and other matters related to your health.
  • Follow Treatment Plan: You are responsible for following the treatment plan recommended by your healthcare provider, including taking medications as prescribed.
  • Express Concerns: You are responsible for asking questions when you do not understand information or instructions and for expressing any concerns about your ability to follow the proposed treatment plan.
  • Hospital Rules: You are responsible for following hospital rules and regulations concerning patient care and conduct.
  • Respect Others: You are responsible for being considerate of the rights of other patients and hospital personnel and for being respectful of the property of other persons and the hospital.
  • Financial Obligations: You are responsible for fulfilling the financial obligations associated with your care promptly.
