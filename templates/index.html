About Arvindu Hospitals

Established in 2005, Arvindu Hospitals has grown to become one of the leading multi-specialty healthcare providers with three branches in Patna, Chapra, and Jamui. Our mission is to deliver excellent medical services with compassion and care.

With over 100 experienced doctors and 500 dedicated staff, we serve more than 10,000 patients every month. Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and the latest medical technologies to provide the best healthcare services.
