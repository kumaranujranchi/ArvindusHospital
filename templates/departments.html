Departments & Specialties

Explore our comprehensive range of medical specialties and services designed to provide you with complete healthcare solutions.

Our Medical Departments

At Arvindu Hospitals, we offer specialized care across various medical disciplines, each equipped with advanced technology and expert healthcare professionals.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for heart conditions, including preventive care, interventional procedures, and cardiac rehabilitation.

  • Cardiac Consultation
  • ECG & Echocardiography
  • Angiography & Angioplasty
  • Cardiac Surgery

Neurology

Our Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

  • Neurological Consultation
  • EEG & EMG
  • Stroke Management
  • Neurosurgery

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics Department focuses on conditions affecting the musculoskeletal system, providing treatments for bone, joint, ligament, and muscle disorders.

  • Orthopedic Consultation
  • Joint Replacement
  • Sports Injuries
  • Spine Surgery

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics Department provides comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents, from preventive care to treatment of complex pediatric conditions.

  • Well-Child Visits
  • Vaccinations
  • Pediatric Specialties
  • Pediatric Surgery

Ophthalmology

Our Ophthalmology Department specializes in eye care, offering comprehensive eye examinations, diagnosis, and treatment of eye disorders and diseases.

  • Eye Examinations
  • Cataract Surgery
  • Glaucoma Treatment
  • LASIK Surgery

Dermatology

Our Dermatology Department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of conditions related to the skin, hair, and nails, from common skin problems to complex dermatological diseases.

  • Skin Consultations
  • Allergy Testing
  • Cosmetic Dermatology
  • Dermatological Surgery

Gynecology

Our Gynecology Department offers comprehensive women's health services, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments for reproductive health issues.

  • Women's Health Check-ups
  • Obstetric Care
  • Gynecological Surgery
  • Fertility Services

Urology

Our Urology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of urinary tract and reproductive system disorders in men and urinary tract disorders in women.

  • Urological Consultations
  • Kidney Stone Treatment
  • Prostate Care
  • Urological Surgery

ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)

Our ENT Department provides diagnosis and treatment for conditions affecting the ears, nose, throat, head, and neck, from common infections to complex surgical cases.

  • ENT Consultations
  • Hearing Tests
  • Sinus Treatments
  • ENT Surgery

Dental

Our Dental Department offers comprehensive dental care services, from routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced dental procedures and surgeries.

  • Dental Check-ups
  • Cosmetic Dentistry
  • Root Canal Treatment
  • Orthodontics

Special Programs & Centers of Excellence

Advanced healthcare initiatives and specialized centers at Arvindu Hospitals

Cardiac Care Center

Our specialized Cardiac Care Center is equipped with state-of-the-art technology for comprehensive heart care, from prevention to advanced interventions and rehabilitation programs. Our team of cardiologists and cardiac surgeons work together to provide the best outcomes for patients with heart conditions.

Diabetes Management Program

Our comprehensive Diabetes Management Program offers personalized care for patients with diabetes, including regular monitoring, diet counseling, lifestyle modifications, and the latest treatment options. Our multidisciplinary team works together to help patients effectively manage their condition.

Rehabilitation Center

Our Rehabilitation Center provides comprehensive services for patients recovering from surgery, injury, or illness. Our team of physical therapists, occupational therapists, and rehabilitation specialists work together to create personalized recovery plans to help patients regain function and independence.

Mother & Child Care Center

Our Mother & Child Care Center provides comprehensive care for expectant mothers and children, from prenatal care and delivery to pediatric healthcare. Our team of obstetricians, gynecologists, pediatricians, and specialized nurses work together to ensure the health and well-being of mothers and their children.

Diagnostic & Laboratory Services

Advanced diagnostic capabilities for accurate and timely results

Imaging Services

  • X-Ray: Digital X-ray services for bone, chest, and abdominal imaging
  • CT Scan: Advanced CT scanning for detailed cross-sectional images
  • MRI: High-resolution magnetic resonance imaging for soft tissues
  • Ultrasound: Comprehensive ultrasound services for various organs
  • Mammography: Digital mammography for breast cancer screening
  • Bone Densitometry: DEXA scanning for bone density measurement

Laboratory Services

  • Hematology: Complete blood count and other blood tests
  • Biochemistry: Comprehensive biochemical analysis
  • Microbiology: Culture and sensitivity tests for infections
  • Immunology: Tests for immune system disorders
  • Pathology: Histopathology and cytology services
  • Molecular Diagnostics: Advanced genetic testing
