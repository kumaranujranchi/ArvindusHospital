Our Medical Departments

Comprehensive healthcare services under one roof

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive diagnosis and treatment for all types of heart diseases. Our team of experienced cardiologists utilizes advanced technologies to deliver the best cardiac care.

  • Echocardiography
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Cardiac Catheterization
  • Coronary Angioplasty
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our neurologists are experts in managing complex neurological conditions.

  • Electroencephalography (EEG)
  • Electromyography (EMG)
  • Neuroimaging
  • Stroke Management
  • Epilepsy Treatment

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic department focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation, and prevention of injuries and diseases of the musculoskeletal system. We offer comprehensive care for bone, joint, and muscle conditions.

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Arthroscopy
  • Fracture Treatment
  • Sports Injury Management
  • Physical Therapy

Pediatrics

Our pediatric department provides specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians are committed to the health and well-being of young patients in a child-friendly environment.

  • Well-Baby Check-ups
  • Immunizations
  • Growth and Development Monitoring
  • Pediatric Emergency Care
  • Childhood Disease Management

Gynecology

Our gynecology department offers comprehensive care for women's reproductive health. Our gynecologists provide preventive care, diagnosis, and treatment for a wide range of gynecological conditions.

  • Preventive Care and Screenings
  • Family Planning
  • Menopause Management
  • Gynecological Surgery
  • Infertility Evaluation

Ophthalmology

Our ophthalmology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders. Our ophthalmologists are skilled in managing various eye conditions and performing surgical procedures to restore and improve vision.

  • Comprehensive Eye Exams
  • Cataract Surgery
  • Glaucoma Treatment
  • Diabetic Eye Care
  • Refractive Surgery

Dermatology

Our dermatology department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of skin, hair, and nail disorders. Our dermatologists provide medical and cosmetic dermatological services for patients of all ages.

  • Skin Disease Treatment
  • Skin Cancer Screening
  • Acne Treatment
  • Dermatologic Surgery
  • Aesthetic Procedures

Urology

Our urology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the urinary tract and male reproductive system. Our urologists utilize advanced techniques to provide comprehensive urological care.

  • Kidney Stone Treatment
  • Urinary Tract Infection Management
  • Prostate Health
  • Urological Surgery
  • Male Infertility Evaluation

ENT

Our ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat) department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the ear, nose, throat, and related structures of the head and neck. Our ENT specialists provide comprehensive care for patients of all ages.

  • Hearing Evaluation
  • Sinus Disease Treatment
  • Voice and Swallowing Disorders
  • Sleep Apnea Management
  • ENT Surgery

Our Medical Facilities

State-of-the-art equipment and infrastructure for optimal care

Laboratory Services

Our advanced laboratory provides comprehensive diagnostic testing services with accurate and timely results. We utilize modern equipment and follow strict quality control protocols.

Radiology Department

Our radiology department offers a full range of imaging services, including X-ray, Ultrasound, CT Scan, and MRI. Our experienced radiologists provide precise interpretations for accurate diagnoses.

In-Patient Facilities

Our in-patient facilities include comfortable rooms with modern amenities to ensure a pleasant stay during treatment. We offer private, semi-private, and general ward options to meet different needs.

Operation Theaters

Our state-of-the-art operation theaters are equipped with advanced surgical equipment and follow strict sterilization protocols. We have dedicated teams of surgeons, anesthesiologists, and OT staff.

Emergency Services

Our emergency department provides round-the-clock care for medical emergencies. Our emergency team is trained to handle critical situations with prompt and efficient response.

Pharmacy

Our in-house pharmacy provides a wide range of medications and medical supplies. Our qualified pharmacists ensure the availability of prescribed medications and provide medication counseling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our departments and services

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM daily. However, visiting hours may vary for different departments and critical care units. We recommend checking with the specific ward or department for their visiting policies.

You can schedule an appointment by calling our appointment desk at +91 123 456 7890, using our online appointment booking system on our website, or visiting our outpatient department in person. Please have your relevant medical records and referrals ready when making an appointment.

We accept most major insurance plans and are empanelled with various corporate and government health schemes. Please contact our insurance desk at +91 987 654 3210 or visit our website for a complete list of accepted insurance providers and cashless facilities.

For your first appointment, please bring your identification, insurance card (if applicable), referral letter (if any), previous medical records, list of current medications, and any recent test reports or imaging studies. This will help our doctors provide you with better care.

You can collect your medical reports from our medical records department during working hours (9:00 AM to 5:00 PM). You will need to show your patient ID card or any valid identification. We also offer a digital report delivery service where reports can be accessed through our patient portal or sent via email upon request.

Our in-patient facilities include private, semi-private, and general ward rooms with amenities such as air conditioning, television, attendant accommodation, Wi-Fi, and 24/7 nursing care. We also have a cafeteria, pharmacy, and prayer room within the hospital premises for patients and their families.
