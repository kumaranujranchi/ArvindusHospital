Departments & Specialties

Comprehensive healthcare services across multiple medical specialties

At Arvindu Hospitals, we offer a wide range of specialized medical services delivered by experienced healthcare professionals using state-of-the-art technology. Our integrated approach ensures comprehensive care for all your health needs.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for all types of heart conditions. Equipped with the latest diagnostic and therapeutic technology, our experienced cardiologists offer personalized treatment plans to improve heart health and quality of life.

Our Services:
  • Non-invasive cardiac testing (ECG, Echocardiography, Stress Tests)
  • Cardiac catheterization and angiography
  • Coronary angioplasty and stenting
  • Pacemaker implantation
  • Management of heart failure, arrhythmias, and coronary artery disease
  • Preventive cardiology and cardiac rehabilitation
Key Specialists:

Dr. Sharma (Interventional Cardiologist)

Dr. Patel (Cardiologist)

Neurology

Our Neurology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. We combine clinical expertise with advanced technology to provide cutting-edge neurological care.

Our Services:
  • Comprehensive neurological evaluation and diagnostics
  • EEG, EMG, and nerve conduction studies
  • Stroke management and rehabilitation
  • Treatment of epilepsy, headaches, and movement disorders
  • Neuromuscular disorder management
  • Cognitive assessment and dementia care
Key Specialists:

Dr. Singh (Neurologist)

Dr. Gupta (Neurologist)

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics Department offers comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions affecting the bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. From sports injuries to joint replacements, our orthopedic specialists provide personalized treatment plans to restore mobility and improve quality of life.

Our Services:
  • Joint replacement surgery (hip, knee, shoulder)
  • Sports medicine and arthroscopic surgery
  • Spine surgery and treatment for back pain
  • Trauma care for fractures and dislocations
  • Treatment of arthritis and other joint conditions
  • Physical therapy and rehabilitation
Key Specialists:

Dr. Kumar (Orthopedic Surgeon)

Dr. Verma (Orthopedic Surgeon)

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics Department provides comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our child-friendly environment and specialized pediatricians ensure the best possible care for your child's physical, emotional, and developmental needs.

Our Services:
  • Well-child visits and routine check-ups
  • Immunizations and preventive care
  • Diagnosis and treatment of acute and chronic conditions
  • Neonatal care and NICU services
  • Pediatric subspecialty consultations
  • Growth and developmental assessments
Key Specialists:

Our Gynecology & Obstetrics Department offers comprehensive women's healthcare services, from routine check-ups to specialized treatments. We provide compassionate care for women at every stage of life, with a focus on preventive health, reproductive care, and maternal-fetal medicine.

Our Services:
  • Routine gynecological examinations and preventive care
  • Prenatal care and high-risk pregnancy management

  • Gynecological surgeries including minimally invasive procedures

  • Treatment of gynecological disorders and infections

  • Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions (acne, eczema, psoriasis)

  • Dermatologic surgery

ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)

  • Pediatric ENT services

Gastroenterology

Our Gastroenterology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the digestive system, including the esophagus, stomach, intestines, liver, and pancreas. Our experienced gastroenterologists use advanced diagnostic and therapeutic techniques to provide comprehensive care for gastrointestinal conditions.

Our Services:
  • Diagnosis and management of digestive disorders
  • Endoscopic procedures (upper endoscopy, colonoscopy)
  • Treatment of liver diseases and hepatitis
  • Management of inflammatory bowel disease
  • Diagnosis and treatment of gastrointestinal cancers
  • Nutritional counseling for digestive health
Key Specialists:

Dr. Tiwari (Gastroenterologist)

  • Cancer survivorship programs
