Work With Us

At Arvindu Hospitals, we believe that our team is our greatest asset. We are committed to creating a supportive and dynamic work environment where healthcare professionals can grow, innovate, and provide exceptional care to our patients.

We offer a range of career opportunities across our three branches in Patna, Chapra, and Jamui. Whether you're an experienced healthcare professional, a fresh graduate, or someone looking to build a career in healthcare administration, we invite you to explore the possibilities with us.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to submit your application

Step 1

Prepare your updated resume and a cover letter tailored to the position you're applying for.

Step 2

Email your application to careers@arvinduhospitals.com with the position title in the subject line.

Step 3

Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for interviews and further assessment.

Note: Please include the following documents with your application:

  • Updated resume/CV
  • Cover letter
  • Copies of educational qualifications
  • Copies of registration certificates (for medical professionals)
  • Experience certificates
  • Two professional references
