Appointment Information

Important information regarding your hospital visit

OPD Timings

Our Outpatient Department (OPD) operates during the following hours:

  • Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Sunday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM (Emergency only)

Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time for registration.

Documents Required

Please bring the following documents with you:

  • Valid photo ID (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, etc.)
  • Previous medical records (if any)
  • Insurance card or policy details (if applicable)
  • List of current medications
  • Recent test reports or scans (if available)

Need Help?

For any assistance with booking or rescheduling your appointment, please contact:

  • Appointment Desk: +91 1234 567891
  • Email: appointment@arvinduhospitals.com
  • WhatsApp: +91 9876 543210

Our appointment coordinators are available during OPD hours to assist you.

Important Note

Once your appointment is scheduled, you will receive a confirmation email with your appointment details. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please provide at least 24 hours notice to accommodate other patients.

For emergency situations, please visit our Emergency Department which is available 24/7, or call our Emergency Helpline at +91 1234 567890.
