June 10, 2023

Arvindu Hospitals Inaugurates State-of-the-Art Cardiac Center in Patna

Arvindu Hospitals has expanded its cardiac care facilities with the inauguration of a new state-of-the-art Cardiac Center at its Patna branch. The center is equipped with the latest technology for diagnostic and interventional cardiology, including a modern catheterization laboratory.

The center will be headed by renowned cardiologist Dr. Sharma and supported by a team of experienced cardiac specialists. This expansion aims to provide comprehensive cardiac care services to patients across Bihar.

May 22, 2023

Five New Specialists Join Arvindu Hospitals Medical Team

Arvindu Hospitals is pleased to announce the addition of five new specialists to its medical team. The new appointments include specialists in neurology, oncology, pediatric gastroenterology, rheumatology, and urology.

These new additions strengthen our multi-specialty capabilities and bring international expertise to our hospitals. The new doctors will practice across our Patna, Chapra, and Jamui branches, expanding our capacity to provide specialized care.

April 15, 2023

Arvindu Hospitals Implements Advanced Robotic Surgery System

In a significant technological advancement, Arvindu Hospitals has introduced a state-of-the-art robotic surgery system at its Patna facility. This minimally invasive surgical technology allows for greater precision, smaller incisions, less pain, and faster recovery times for patients.

The robotic system will be used across multiple specialties including urology, gynecology, and general surgery. Our surgical teams have undergone comprehensive training to utilize this technology effectively.
