About Us

Learn more about Arvindu Hospitals, our mission, vision and values

Our Story

Established in 2005, Arvindu Hospitals began as a small clinic in Patna with a vision to provide quality healthcare services to the people of Bihar. Over the years, we have grown into a renowned multi-specialty hospital network with branches in Patna, Chapra, and Jamui.

Our journey has been fueled by our commitment to excellence, compassion, and innovation in healthcare. Today, Arvindu Hospitals stands as a symbol of trust and quality medical care in the region.

With state-of-the-art medical equipment, highly qualified doctors, and dedicated staff, we strive to deliver world-class healthcare services that are accessible and affordable to all sections of society.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, patient-centered healthcare of the highest quality, combining clinical excellence with compassion, and to continually improve the well-being of the communities we serve.

  • Deliver exceptional patient care
  • Advance medical knowledge through research
  • Train the next generation of healthcare professionals
  • Serve our community with dedication

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Bihar, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and compassionate care, making quality healthcare accessible to all.

  • Excellence in medical care
  • Innovation in healthcare delivery
  • Accessibility for all patients
  • Leadership in healthcare standards

Our Core Values
