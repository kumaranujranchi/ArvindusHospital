About Us

Learn more about Arvindu Hospitals, our mission, vision and values

Our Story

Established in 2005, Arvindu Hospitals began as a small clinic in Patna with a vision to provide quality healthcare services to the people of Bihar. Over the years, we have grown into a renowned multi-specialty hospital network with branches in Patna, Chapra, and Jamui.

Our journey has been fueled by our commitment to excellence, compassion, and innovation in healthcare. Today, Arvindu Hospitals stands as a symbol of trust and quality medical care in the region.

With state-of-the-art medical equipment, highly qualified doctors, and dedicated staff, we strive to deliver world-class healthcare services that are accessible and affordable to all sections of society.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive, accessible, and high-quality healthcare services with compassion and respect for all patients. We are committed to:

  • Delivering patient-centered care with empathy and integrity
  • Utilizing advanced medical technology for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment
  • Making quality healthcare accessible and affordable to all sections of society
  • Continuously improving through research, education, and innovation

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare provider in Eastern India, recognized for clinical excellence, patient-centered care, and innovative medical practices. We aspire to:

  • Become a center of excellence for specialized medical services
  • Expand our reach to serve more communities with quality healthcare
  • Be at the forefront of medical innovation and research
  • Set new standards in healthcare delivery through continuous improvement
  • Create a healthier community through preventive care and education

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our actions and decisions every day

Compassion

We treat each patient with kindness, empathy, and respect, recognizing their individual needs and concerns.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in clinical care, service quality, and professional development.

Teamwork

We work collaboratively across disciplines to provide integrated, coordinated care for our patients.

Integrity

We adhere to high ethical standards and principles in all our actions and decisions.

Innovation

We embrace advancements in medical technology and continuously seek innovative solutions to improve patient care.

Patient-Centered

We place patients at the center of everything we do, respecting their dignity, preferences, and values.
