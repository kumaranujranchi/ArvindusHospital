Departments & Specialties

Comprehensive healthcare services across multiple medical specialties

At Arvindu Hospitals, we offer a wide range of specialized medical services delivered by experienced healthcare professionals using state-of-the-art technology. Our integrated approach ensures comprehensive care for all your health needs.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology Department provides comprehensive care for all types of heart conditions. Equipped with the latest diagnostic and therapeutic technology, our experienced cardiologists offer personalized treatment plans to improve heart health and quality of life.

Our Services:
  • Non-invasive cardiac testing (ECG, Echocardiography, Stress Tests)
  • Cardiac catheterization and angioplasty
  • Pacemaker implantation
  • Heart failure management
  • Preventive cardiology and risk assessment
Book Appointment

Neurology

Our Neurology Department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves. Our neurologists use advanced diagnostic techniques to provide accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.

Our Services:
  • Stroke care and rehabilitation
  • Epilepsy management
  • Movement disorders (Parkinson's disease)
  • Headache and migraine treatment
  • Neurological diagnostic testing (EEG, EMG)
Book Appointment

Orthopedics

Our Orthopedics Department provides comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions, including bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles. We offer both surgical and non-surgical treatments to restore mobility and reduce pain.

Our Services:
  • Joint replacement surgery (hip, knee, shoulder)
  • Sports medicine and injury treatment
  • Fracture care and trauma surgery
  • Spine surgery and treatment
  • Arthroscopic surgery
Book Appointment

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics Department provides specialized healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians are trained to handle the unique medical needs of growing children in a child-friendly environment.

Our Services:
  • Well-child checkups and vaccinations
  • Pediatric emergency care
  • Growth and development monitoring
  • Treatment of childhood illnesses
  • Pediatric surgery and specialized care
Book Appointment

Gynecology & Obstetrics

Our Gynecology & Obstetrics Department provides comprehensive women's healthcare services, from routine gynecological care to high-risk pregnancy management and delivery services.

Our Services:
  • Prenatal care and delivery services
  • High-risk pregnancy management
  • Gynecological surgery (minimally invasive)
  • Family planning and contraception
  • Menopause management
Book Appointment

Emergency Care

Our Emergency Department operates 24/7 to provide immediate medical care for urgent and life-threatening conditions. Our emergency medicine specialists are equipped to handle all types of medical emergencies.

Our Services:
  • 24/7 emergency medical care
  • Trauma and critical care
  • Advanced life support
  • Emergency surgery
  • Ambulance services
